Gitanjali

Tagore's Gitanjali (NO. 50) offers a spiritual lesson via a story about the importance of compassion, love, and sacrifice. God, dressed as a poor beggar, solicited charity from the poet beggar, who had been out and about soliciting donations because he, too, had suffered the pains of poverty.

You Can is a motivational and self-help book written by George M. Adams. The book offers practical insights and strategies to help individuals achieve personal and professional success.
